
# Portfolio Evidence Note

## Project title

Human-in-the-Loop AI Workflow Evaluator

## Application relevance

This app demonstrates that biomedical and scientific AI experience is transferable to operational analytical environments.

Although the training comes from biomedical and scientific AI, the underlying skills are directly transferable to high-stakes AI workflows, working similarly with noisy, incomplete, heterogeneous, and high-dimensional data, where models must support human judgement rather than replace it.

## What the app demonstrates

The app demonstrates the ability to:

- define the real decision point

- map noisy data flows

- identify where AI can add value

- build a small proof of concept

- compare AI outputs with a baseline process

- evaluate model confidence and completeness

- stress-test failure modes

- visualise results in 2D and 3D

- apply human approval gates

- document an audit trail

- explain model limitations clearly

## Responsible AI principles

The app is built around responsible AI principles:

- AI supports human judgement

- low-confidence outputs are escalated

- incomplete evidence is flagged

- risk is visible to the user

- every decision is auditable

- outputs are never treated as final without human review

- the system is bounded and explainable

## Relevance to AI, NLP, and workflow-oriented systems

The app provides a safe, non-sensitive demonstration of text classification, workflow triage, readiness scoring, failure-mode analysis, and human-in-the-loop AI. These are directly relevant to applied AI systems using natural language processing, large language models, retrieval-aware workflows, and agentic or workflow-oriented AI.
